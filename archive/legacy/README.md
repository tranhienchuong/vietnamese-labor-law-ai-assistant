# Legacy Archive

This directory keeps exploratory or superseded artifacts that are not part of the thesis-aligned Vietnamese Labor Law GraphRAG QA pipeline.

Archived material may include:

- standalone LLM-as-Judge or RAGAS experiments,
- older 69-query benchmark reports,
- conflicting benchmark summaries,
- stale exploratory retrieval or end-to-end outputs,
- earlier two-document corpus metadata and chunks.

The official current artifacts remain in their active locations:

- `artifacts/chunks/legal_chunks_enriched.jsonl`
- `artifacts/index/current.json`
- `artifacts/graph/legal_graph_build_summary.json`
- `artifacts/evaluation/golden_benchmark_100_extended.jsonl`

Use archived files only for provenance or comparison. They are not the final evaluation method or active corpus source.
